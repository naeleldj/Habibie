Bacharuddin Jusuf Habibie

Genius Engineer, Former Indonesian President, and the "Father of Technology". Explore the life of a visionary who shaped a nation's technological future.

Academic Journey

From Bandung to Germany, his academic path was marked by brilliance and led to a discovery that changed aviation safety forever.

Graduation from ITB

Began his journey in engineering at the prestigious Bandung Institute of Technology (ITB).

Advanced Studies in Germany

Continued his education in Germany, delving deep into aerospace engineering and technology.

Doctorate with Summa Cum Laude

Earned his doctorate with the highest possible distinction, a testament to his exceptional intellect and dedication.

Discovery of the "Habibie Factor"

Developed a groundbreaking formula to calculate crack propagation in aircraft structures. This discovery, earning him the nickname "Mr. Crack," is now a global standard in the airline industry.

A Dual-Continent Career

Habibie's professional life was defined by pivotal leadership roles in both Germany's advanced aerospace sector and Indonesia's burgeoning technology industry.
